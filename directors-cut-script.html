Pour Yourself In

Client Directors Cut
Duration ~30 sec
Talent Two Females, Two Males
Shoot Date 4 July
Scene 01
Establishing / Exterior
5–6 sec

B-roll of the venue exterior. Signage, architecture, atmosphere. The four approach — dressed up, relaxed. Walking toward the entrance and into the venue. No dialogue yet. Tone setting only.

Scene 02
Interior Entrance
4–5 sec

As they step inside, the two girls look at each other. They move further in, smiling.

Dialogue
Girl 1 — "So what we watching?"
Girl 2 nods, looking to the guys.
Guy 1 — "It's a surprise."
Guy 2 — "Drinks first?"
Scene 03
The Pour
8 sec

Cut to a close-up of a wine glass being filled — slow, deliberate, cinematic. Pull back to b-roll of the full table setup. Wine bottles, glasses, the spread. Warm light. Feels indulgent.

Scene 04
The Cheers
7 sec

B-roll of all four raising their glasses. Camera angle from below looking up — glasses catching the light overhead. Slow motion. Relaxed, natural energy. Laughter without sound. Cinematic and celebratory.

Scene 05
The Experience
10 sec

The four settle into their seats as the camera steadily dollies right, following them as they get comfortable. The film begins.

  • B-roll — hands reaching into popcorn
  • Laughing reactions, nudging each other
  • Scared faces, gripping their seats
  • Fully immersed, lost in the screen
  • Close-up beauty shots — faces lit by screen glow, warm and intimate
  • Wider shots — heavily vignetted, circle crop. Nostalgic, keyhole feel
Scene 06
The Staff Reaction
3 sec

Whip pan to a staff member nearby — arms folded, slow head shake, half a smirk. The "darn kids" energy. Doesn't say a word. Doesn't need to.

Scene 07
End Card
2 sec

Shallow focus close-up of the four in the background — heavily blurred, screen glow washing over them. Warm, atmospheric bokeh.
